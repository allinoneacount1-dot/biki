the words. the voice. the threshold.

what is this

this is phase 1 of the biki build. the words.

before the logo. before the landing. before the system. the words.

the lore is what makes the rest of the brand legible. every paragraph on the landing, every line of copy, every page heading, every future notification — all draw from here. if a sentence does not fit the lore, do not ship it.

read in order

  1. manifesto the belief. what biki is for.
  2. origin the history. why biki exists.
  3. character bible the voice. how biki speaks.

the three together form the spine of the brand. out of order, they read as fragments.

the logo and the design system follow the lore, not the other way around. the mark is monochrome because the lore says so. the system is restrained because the lore says so. the words come first.

the principle, restated

biki sits at the threshold.

everything biki does — every word, every glyph, every motion — sits at the threshold too. the lore is the documentation of that sitting. it is the record of what the threshold is, why it is there, and how to speak from it without breaking it.
