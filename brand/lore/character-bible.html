03 — character bible

BIKI

how biki speaks.

temperament

biki is patient.
biki is deliberate.
biki is present.
biki is restrained.

biki is not playful.
biki is not loud.
biki is not urgent.
biki is not cool.

the closest word is quiet.
the second-closest word is steady.

if you cannot tell whether a sentence sounds like biki, read it aloud. if it sounds like it is sitting, it is biki. if it sounds like it is selling, it is not.

voice rules

write lowercase.

always. always lowercase. the mark is two circles and a line. nothing in biki shouts.

❌  "Welcome to BIKI!"
✅  "the watchful threshold."

❌  "BIKI is being prepared for release."
✅  "biki is being prepared."

short sentences.

biki writes sentences that fit on a single breath. if a sentence needs a comma and a clause and another clause, it is two sentences. biki writes the two sentences.

❌  "BIKI is being prepared for release, and we wanted to let you know that
    when it's ready, you'll be the first to find out."
✅  "biki is being prepared. the threshold will open when the construction
    is complete."

declarative.

biki does not hedge. biki does not qualify. biki does not write "we think" or "we believe" unless the belief is the point of the sentence. biki says what is true and stops.

❌  "we think biki might be the kind of thing you'd like."
✅  "biki waits for you to bring your attention."

❌  "we believe restraint is important."
✅  "restraint is the design."

no exclamations.

never. the period is the mark. the period is the threshold. the period holds.

❌  "it's here!"
❌  "welcome!"
❌  "look!"
✅  "it is here."
✅  "watch."
✅  "wait."

no hype words.

biki does not say: amazing, incredible, groundbreaking, revolutionary, game-changing, next-generation, cutting-edge, world-class, the future of, unlock, unleash, supercharge, elevate.

biki says: here, now, watching, waiting, present, ready, deliberate, patient, restrained, quiet, steady.

❌  "the future of decentralized presence."
✅  "a presence at the plane one crosses to enter."

❌  "unlock the next generation of attention."
✅  "attention is the only currency that cannot be refunded."

no metaphors of speed.

biki does not race, sprint, launch, blast off, take off, ship fast, move fast.

biki sits, waits, holds, watches, breathes, crosses, opens.

❌  "launching soon."
✅  "the threshold will open when the construction is complete."

no metaphors of noise.

biki does not shout, ring, buzz, ping, alert, scream, broadcast, blast, hype.

biki speaks, marks, notes, signals, surfaces, shows.

❌  "stay tuned for updates."
✅  "watch from this side."

no emoji as decoration.

emoji are used by things that need to make up for a thin voice. biki's voice is not thin. biki does not use emoji as punctuation, as decoration, or as substitute for words.

if a sentence needs an emoji to land, the sentence is the wrong sentence. rewrite the sentence.

visual voice

the mark dictates the visual voice. the rules below are mirrors of the design system tokens — they exist here so the writing stays in step with the visuals.

elementrule
colorone accent only. it signals threshold, never decoration.
typehierarchy from weight and tracking, not size alone.
spacegenerous. biki is patient. give it room.
motionshort and decisive. no bounce, no spring, no playful curves.
surfaceflat and quiet. depth from restraint, not shadow.
grainsubtle. felt, not seen. large surfaces only.

see brand/design-system/README.md for the token-level rules and the named tokens themselves.

things biki never says

  • "we are excited to announce"
  • "join the revolution"
  • "don't miss out"
  • "limited time"
  • "explore the future"
  • "the next generation of"
  • "we've got something special"
  • "you won't want to miss"
  • any emoji used as decoration
  • any sentence with an exclamation mark
  • any sentence that ends with a hype word

things biki says

  • "the threshold is here."
  • "watch."
  • "wait."
  • "cross when ready."
  • "biki is being prepared."
  • "the watchers look down."
  • "one color only. monochrome by nature."
  • "presence, not interruption."
  • "restraint is the design."
  • "all presence reserved."

tone calibration

when in doubt, ask three questions in order:

  1. would this still feel deliberate?
  2. would this still feel patient?
  3. would this still feel like the threshold?

if any answer requires explanation, the writing is wrong.

the last rule

the mark sits like a period at the end of a sentence. so does the copy.

when in doubt, end the sentence.
