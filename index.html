01 — the principle

BIKI is built on a single idea: presence, not interruption.

Where most things demand your attention, BIKI waits. It watches the threshold — the moment before action — and decides whether to engage.

This is not a tool that shouts. This is a tool that sits at the entrance and lets you decide when to cross.

The geometry of the mark is intentional. Two circles, vertically stacked, watch downward. A single line marks the plane. The mark is monochrome because attention is monochrome. The mark is geometric because deliberation is geometric.

02 — the mark

the mark is two watchers above a threshold.

the circles are vertical, not horizontal — they read as principle, not face.

the threshold line is narrower than the mark — it is the floor, not the pedestal.

one color only. monochrome by nature. color is applied through context, not through the mark.

03 — the threshold

BIKI is being prepared.

The threshold will open when the construction is complete.
